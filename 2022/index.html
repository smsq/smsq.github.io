Eighth Statistical Mechanics of Soft Matter Conference

The Eighth SM2 Meeting was held at Monash University on 14-15 July, 2022.



SM2 is a local discussion meeting on all aspects of the equilibrium and nonequilibrium statistical mechanics of soft condensed matter, from fundamentals to applications, relating to liquids, colloids, polymers, gels, biological molecules, liquid crystals and other forms of soft matter. The meeting will consist of a single stream of talks and (most likely) a virtual poster session.

SM2 will be preceded by a statistical mechanics winter school, to be held on 12-13 July.

Details

This year, the meeting will be held in hybrid mode, with talks given in person and via Zoom.

A provisional programme is now available.

Registration

Registration is free. Please email the conference organisers if you have not previously registered and are interested in participating in either the winter school or the conference: smsq.meeting@gmail.com.

Note for face-to-face participants: Face-to-face participants will need to be fully vaccinated. A small registration fee might be charged to cover catering of morning and afternoon tea. This will be paid in cash at the start of the conference.

If you wish to be added to our mailing list please send an email to: smsq.meeting@gmail.com.

Winter School

The winter school will be held on 12-13 July, 2022, and will consist of lectures and hands-on workshops in statistical mechanics. It is targeted at research students and postdocs, and registration is free. Note: please email the conference organisers if you have not previously registered and are interested in participating.
