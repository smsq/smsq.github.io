Statistical Mechanics of Soft Matter 2015

To be held at Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus, Melbourne, November 30 - December 1, 2015.

This will be an informal conference on the fundamentals and applications of equilibrium and non-equilibrium statistical mechanics (particularly computational statistical mechanics) relating to simple and complex liquids, polymers, biological materials and other forms of soft matter.

Talks will be for 20-25 minutes depending on the number of speakers, with ample time for discussion. There will be no conference fee, but accommodation or meals will not be covered. Tea breaks and coffee vouchers will be offered.

Discounted rates for accommodation with our University partners may be available, details to come.

If you are interested in attending, we would appreciate receiving a title and an abstract by Friday October 30, 2015. Student and postdoctoral presentations/posters, as well as reports on work in progress, are strongly encouraged.
